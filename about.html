About Us

Welcome to Our Goshala

Our Goshala is dedicated to the **rescue, care, and well-being of cows**, ensuring they live in a **safe and nurturing environment**. We believe in **protecting and preserving** these sacred beings while promoting **sustainable and ethical practices** in animal care.

Our Mission

Our mission is to **rescue, shelter, and rehabilitate cows**, ensuring they receive **proper nutrition, medical care, and love**. We strive to create an **eco-friendly and sustainable** environment where cows can live freely and happily.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every cow is treated with **compassion and dignity**. Through community involvement, education, and sustainable agricultural practices, we aim to make **Goshala a beacon of ethical animal welfare**.

Our Commitment

At Our Goshala, we are committed to:

  • Providing **safe shelter** for rescued cows.
  • Ensuring **veterinary care and a nutritious diet** for their well-being.
  • Developing **sustainable farming** methods using cow-based organic products.
  • Raising awareness about **ethical and compassionate animal treatment**.

Our Impact

Over the years, we have successfully:

  • **Rescued & sheltered 57+ cows** from harsh conditions.
  • **Developed 17,000+ Sq. Ft. of fodder land** to provide fresh food.
  • **Provided medical care** to over 120+ cows regularly.
  • **Grown a strong community** of 2800+ dedicated volunteers.

Community & Volunteer Support

Our success is powered by our **dedicated volunteers, donors, and community members** who share our vision. Whether through **time, resources, or spreading awareness**, every contribution makes a significant impact.

Join Our Movement

If you wish to **volunteer, donate, or learn more**, we invite you to be a part of **Our Goshala family**. Together, we can create a **compassionate and sustainable** future for cows.

Thank you for supporting Our Goshala. Together, we make a difference!
